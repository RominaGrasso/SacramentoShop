🍣 🌙 Premium Night Experience

About this experience

Experience Colonia at its most exclusive. Stay at La Misión, a charming boutique posada in the heart of the historic quarter, where Mercedes, the owner, will personally welcome you and prepare a delightful homemade breakfast. At the entrance, the blooming hydrangeas — planted by her grandmother — tell a story that has been part of the house for generations. Enjoy a world-class sushi dinner at SIO, one of the most renowned culinary experiences in Colonia. Wake up to a warm and personalized morning, and complete your night with a guided walking tour through the city's most iconic streets.

How it works

  • 🏛 Arrival at La Misión: Check in at your boutique posada in the historic district.
  • 🍣 Dinner at SIO: Enjoy a premium sushi experience with signature dishes and fresh ingredients.
  • 🛏 Overnight stay: Relax in a charming colonial-style room.
  • ☕ Breakfast included: Start your morning with a personalized breakfast.
  • 🎁 Guided walking tour: Discover Colonia with a local guide.
