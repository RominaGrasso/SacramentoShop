🚤 🌅 Corporate Sunset Boat Experience

About this experience

Enjoy an exclusive sunset boat experience along the river with Captain Alejandro, designed for corporate groups and private events. Relax on a private boat while the sun sets over the water, creating a unique and unforgettable atmosphere.

After the navigation, continue the evening with a premium dinner at Casa Petit by Mercedes, where you will experience a refined and intimate dining setting.

How it works

  • 🚕 Arrival & transfer: A private transfer will take you to the departure point.
  • 🚤 Sunset navigation: Enjoy a private boat experience with Captain Alejandro.
  • 🌅 Golden hour: Relax and enjoy drinks while watching the sunset over the river.
  • 🍽 Dinner at Casa Petit: Continue the experience with a premium dinner.
  • 🎁 Complimentary guided tour: Discover Colonia with a local guide.

USD 150 per person

Minimum 12 people
