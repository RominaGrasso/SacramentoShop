🏛 🌙 La Misión Night Experience

About this experience

Step back in time with a unique overnight stay in Colonia’s historic quarter. Stay at La Misión, a charming posada located right in front of Plaza Mayor. Enjoy a refined dinner at Bruma, just around the corner, and wake up to a cozy breakfast. Complete your experience with a guided walking tour through the most iconic streets of Colonia.

How it works

  • 🏛 Arrival at La Misión: Check in directly at your posada in the historic district.
  • 🍽 Dinner at Bruma: Enjoy a curated dinner just a short walk away.
  • 🛏 Overnight stay: Relax in a colonial-style room surrounded by history.
  • ☕ Breakfast included: Start your morning with a personalized breakfast.
  • 🎁 Guided walking tour: Discover Colonia with a local guide.
