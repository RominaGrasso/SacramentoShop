🏛 🌙 La Misión Night Experience

About this experience

Step back in time with a unique overnight stay in Colonia’s historic quarter. Stay at La Misión, a charming boutique posada in front of Plaza Mayor, where Mercedes, the owner, will personally welcome you and prepare a warm homemade breakfast. At the entrance, the hydrangeas planted by her grandmother tell a story that has been part of the house for generations. Just steps away, enjoy an intimate dining experience at Bruma, where Chef Marcelo will personally welcome you and guide you through his cuisine. Complete your night with a guided walking tour through Colonia’s most iconic streets.

How it works

  • 🏛 Arrival at La Misión: Check in directly at your posada in the historic district.
  • 🍽 Dinner at Bruma: Enjoy a curated dinner just a short walk away.
  • 🛏 Overnight stay: Relax in a colonial-style room surrounded by history.
  • ☕ Breakfast included: Start your morning with a personalized breakfast.
  • 🎁 Guided walking tour: Discover Colonia with a local guide.
