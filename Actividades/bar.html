About this experience

Experience Colonia’s nightlife like a local with a curated bar hopping tour. Visit iconic spots such as Casa Viera, La Chopería and Barbot, enjoying signature cocktails, local beers and a vibrant social atmosphere in the historic quarter. After the tour, continue the night with optional drinks at Café del Río and finish dancing at one of the city’s top nightclubs.
