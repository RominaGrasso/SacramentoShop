About this experience

Enjoy a unique overnight experience in Colonia del Sacramento combining gastronomy, comfort, and culture. Start your evening with a delicious dinner at Las Liebres, then relax in a cozy apartment near Plaza de Toros. Wake up to a fresh breakfast and continue your experience with a visit to the iconic bullring — tickets included.

How it works

  • 🚕 Personalized arrival: A local guide will welcome you and take you to your apartment, giving you time to settle in and start your experience comfortably.
  • 🍽 Dinner experience: Enjoy a curated dinner at a selected local restaurant.
  • 🛏 Overnight stay: Spend the night in a comfortable apartment near Plaza de Toros.
  • ☕ Breakfast included: Start your morning with a fresh breakfast.
  • 🏟 Plaza de Toros visit: Explore the iconic bullring with tickets included.
