About this experience

Explore Colonia del Sacramento’s UNESCO World Heritage historic district with a knowledgeable local guide. Walk through cobblestone streets, discover hidden corners, and hear the fascinating stories behind the city’s Portuguese and Spanish past.

🎥 Watch Reel on @_sacramentoadventures

How it works

  • 🏛 Meeting point: Plaza de la Basílica (Historic Quarter)
  • 🚶 Guided walking tour: Explore the historic streets and learn about Colonia’s past with a local expert
  • 📸 Hidden gems: Discover secret corners and best photo spots
  • 🌅 End of tour: Finish in the historic area with recommendations to continue exploring
