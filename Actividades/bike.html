About this experience

Explore Colonia del Sacramento by bicycle with a relaxed guided tour. Ride through the Historic Quarter, the Rambla waterfront and scenic local neighborhoods while discovering the city’s history and culture. A perfect experience for active travelers.

How it works

  • 🚴 Meeting point: Central location in Colonia (details after booking)
  • 🗺 Guided bike tour: Ride through historic streets, waterfront areas and local neighborhoods
  • 📸 Stops: Scenic viewpoints and photo spots along the way
  • 🍩 Local touch: Enjoy a traditional torta frita during the experience
  • 🌅 End of tour: Return to starting point or continue exploring
