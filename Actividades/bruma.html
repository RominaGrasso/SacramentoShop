About this experience

Enjoy a unique gastronomic experience at Bruma, a boutique restaurant located in the heart of Colonia’s Historic Quarter. Surrounded by cobblestone streets and charming colonial houses, Bruma offers an intimate and relaxed atmosphere. Led by Chef Marcelo, the restaurant features a carefully crafted international menu with high-quality ingredients and creative dishes. This experience is the perfect complement after a guided tour — a moment to relax, enjoy great food, and soak in the essence of Colonia.
