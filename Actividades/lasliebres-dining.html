About this experience

Enjoy a lunch or dinner experience at Las Liebres, a boutique restaurant in Colonia del Sacramento. This activity focuses on the gastronomic menu and restaurant atmosphere, with private transfer included. Andres, the manager, will be waiting to welcome your group at Las Liebres. You can choose your preferred meal format when you create your order.

🎥 Watch Reel on @_sacramentoadventures

How it works

  • 🚐 Private pick-up: Transfer from your hotel or the bus terminal.
  • 🍽 Lunch or dinner: Choose your menu at Las Liebres when creating your order.
  • 🌿 Restaurant setting: Boutique dining experience in a relaxed atmosphere.
  • 🏨 Return: Private drop-off back to your hotel or terminal.

Dining experiences from USD 65

Private transport is billed by vehicle: USD 30 total for 1–4 guests (one vehicle); USD 60 total for 5–8 guests (two vehicles). Add one order per guest — the summary and total update automatically.

What's included

  • ✔ Lunch or dinner menu at Las Liebres (depending on package)
  • ✔ Boutique restaurant atmosphere
  • ✔ Welcome coordination at the venue
  • ✔ Private round-trip transfer (hotel or terminal; see summary)

Create your order

Choose lunch or dinner and menu style (Viñedo or Ceibo), then add preferences.

El guía acompaña al grupo desde el punto de encuentro hasta Las Liebres y coordina los tiempos del servicio para que la experiencia fluya sin esperas. También ayuda con la organización en mesa y necesidades del grupo durante toda la actividad, y acompaña la coordinación del regreso.

After your reservation, a member of our support team will contact you to guide you through the details of your experience and answer any questions before your visit.
