About this experience

Step back in time and experience Colonia del Sacramento in a unique way. Dress in traditional colonial attire and capture unforgettable photos in a historic setting. Then, enjoy an authentic Uruguayan chivito — one of the country’s most iconic dishes — and finish your journey with a visit to the iconic Plaza de Toros. A perfect combination of culture, gastronomy and history.

How it works

  • 🎭 Meeting point: We welcome you at our costume store located in Paseo del Sol Gallery.
  • 🍽 Lunch experience: Enjoy your meal at El Refugio restaurant, just 2 blocks away from Paseo del Sol.
  • 🏛 Plaza de Toros visit: After lunch, a private transfer will take you to the iconic Plaza de Toros. Tickets are included.
  • 🚗 Return: At the end of the experience, your private transfer will take you back to your hotel or terminal.
