🔥🚤 Asado & Boat Experience

About this experience

Experience the true essence of Uruguay with a unique combination of gastronomy and nature. Enjoy an authentic asado prepared by Andres in El Refugio restaurant. You will taste premium meats, local wine, and a relaxed atmosphere. Then, embark on a scenic boat tour along the Río de la Plata, discovering Colonia from a completely different perspective.

How it works

  • 📍 Meeting point: Historic Quarter (shared after booking)
  • 🔥 Asado experience: Enjoy a traditional Uruguayan BBQ prepared by Andres in "El Refugio" with premium meats and wine.
  • 🚤 Boat tour: Navigate the Río de la Plata and enjoy stunning views of Colonia from the water with captain Alejandro.
  • 🌅 Relax & enjoy: Perfect moment to unwind, take photos and experience the sunset.
  • 🚶 All activities are within walking distance

USD 70 per person

What's included

  • ✔ Traditional Uruguayan asado
  • ✔ Premium meat selection
  • ✔ Wine or beverage included
  • ✔ Boat tour experience
  • ✔ Local host (Alejandro)
  • ✔ Transfer included
